SESAME_CHICKEN

DESCRIPTION

SIMPLE SAUCY CHICKEN OVER RICE

INGREDIENTS

CHICKEN

  • 1_EGG
  • 2_TBSP_CORNSTARCH
  • 1_PINCH_SALT//PEPPER
  • 2_TBSP_COOKINGOIL
  • 1_LB_BONELESSCHICKENTHIGHS

SAUCE

  • 1/4_CUP_SOYSAUCE
  • 2_TBSP_WATER
  • 1_TBSP_SESAMEOIL
  • 3_TBSP_BROWNSUGAR
  • 1_TBSP_RICEVINEGAR
  • 1_TSP_GRATEDGINGER
  • 2_CLOVES_GARLIC[MINCED]
  • 1_TBSP_SESAMESEEDS
  • 1/2_TBSP_CORNSTARCH

MISC

  • 4_CUPS_RICE
  • 2_WHOLE_GREENONIONS

STEPS

  1. prep sauce: stir soysauce + water + sesameoil + brownsugar + ricevingar + ginger + garlic + cornstarch + sesameseeds
  2. in large bowl: whisk egg + 2tbsp corntarch + salt/pep
  3. trim fat from chicken, cut into small 1in pieces, add to mix
  4. add cookingoil to skillet + heat
  5. swirl oil to coat surface when hot, adding chicken (spread chicken into single layer)
  6. cook chicken until golden brown, flip (limit excess stirring)
  7. pour sauce in when cooked
  8. sauce will simmer, stir chicken (gently) until thickened
  9. remove from heat + serve
